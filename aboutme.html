About Me

Hey, I’m Devanshi Rajyaguru — the one pulling all the strings behind the scenes. From big ideas to tiny details, I’ve been the brains, the hands, and sometimes even the coffee runner of every single project.

I’ve been in this wild, wonderful world of social media and marketing for over two years now. And for more than a year, I’ve been riding solo as a freelancer. And let me tell you, freelancing? It’s the crash course that teaches you everything — fast.

I’ve done it all: strategy, copy, production, post-production, client calls, feedback loops, accounts — you name it. But not in a burnt-out, “I do too much” way. More like, I’ve become every department for my clients, and I’m genuinely grateful for that. It’s made me sharper, smarter, and way better at what I do.

Freelancing taught me how to read a client’s mind, understand an audience’s scroll behavior, and turn creative chaos into structured strategy.

At the core, I’m a creative soul — someone who loves dreaming up content that makes people pause, laugh, or nod in agreement. And marketing? That’s my playground. It’s where my brain goes full turbo.

This site is just a mashup of everything I’ve learned, created, and loved doing in the world of marketing. So binge around! I promise, it’s worth the watch.
